The European Union (EU) ban on the sale of new petrol and diesel cars from 2035 explained. However about 10% of E-drivers in Germany do not have access to a parking close to their home hence no opportunity to charge their car at an own wallbox. The same applies for 19% who are planning to buy an electric car soon.
Top 4 of 14 reasons NOT to buy an electric car is inconvenience in charging the car. The following Prototype tries to enable wallbox-owners to share their wallbox in a safe and easy way with other E-drivers when the wallbox is not in use by the owners.

Project 1: "ShareCharge"
ShareCharge is safe, fair and benefits wallbox-owners as well as E-drivers in the neighborhood.
